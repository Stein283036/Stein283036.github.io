  1. surrogate/ˈsʌrəɡət/

    For some people, reading travel books is a surrogate for actual travel.

    对某些人来说，读游记可以替代真正的旅行。

    Because she had no children of her own, her friend’s son became a kind of surrogate child to her.

    因为她自己没有孩子，所以她就把朋友的儿子当成了自己的孩子。

  2. obscure[əbsgjʉ́ː]

    not known to many people

    an obscure island in the Pacific

    太平洋中的无名小岛

    an obscure 12th-century mystic

    12 世纪一位鲜为人知的神秘主义者

    not clear and difficult to understand or see

    Official policy has changed, for reasons that remain obscure.

    官方的政策已改变，原因尚不清楚。

    His answers were obscure and confusing.

    他的回答模糊不清，令人费解。

    to prevent something from being seen or heard

    Two new skyscrapers had sprung up, obscuring the view from her window.

    两座新摩天大楼拔地而起，挡住了她窗外的风景。

    The sun was obscured by clouds.

    太阳被云层遮住了。

    to make something difficult to discover and understand

    Managers deliberately obscured the real situation from federal investigators.

    经理们故意对联邦调查人员掩盖真实情况。

  3. mattress /ˈmæt.rəs/ 床垫

    Over the years the mattress has lost its spring.

    几年下来，床垫失去了弹性。

  4. mediation

    调解，调停，斡旋

    Last-minute attempts at mediation failed.

    最后一刻的调解努力失败了。

    In divorce cases where there are children, mediation is preferable to going to court.

    在有孩子的离婚案件中，调解比上法庭更可取。

  5. performance

    工作情况；业绩；表现；工作性能

    He was an experienced player who was always seeking to improve his performance.

    他是一名有经验的选手，不断寻求提高自己的技能。

    High-performance cars (= those that are fast, powerful, and easy to control) are the most expensive.

    性能卓越的车价格最高。

    This was a very impressive performance by the young player, who scored 14 points within the first ten minutes.

    那位年轻球员的表现十分出色，在开场 10 分钟内就得了 14 分。

    She gave a superb performance as Lady Macbeth.

    她出色地扮演了麦克白夫人。

  6. issue

    environmental/ethical/personal issues

    环境／道德／个人问题

    As employers we need to be seen to be addressing (= dealing with) these issues sympathetically.

    作为雇主，我们需要让大家知道我们在处理这些问题时富有同情心。

  7. cold call 陌生电话

  8. home appliances[əˈplaɪ əns] 家用电器

  9. HMD head-mounted display 头戴式显示器

  10. freelancer 自由职业者

    freelancing 自由职业

  11. take on a side hustle | a part-time job 从事副业

  12. pet sitter 宠物保姆

  13. executive producer 导演

  14. cast of characters 演员表

  15. stipulation 规定

    Is there any stipulation concerning the number of people attending the conference?

    参加会议的人数方面有什么明确的要求吗？

    The only stipulation is that candidates must be over the age of 35.

    唯一的规定是候选人必须年满 35 岁。

  16. interchangeably/ˌɪn.t̬ɚˈtʃeɪn.dʒə.bli/ 可互换地

    These garments could be worn interchangeably by men and women.

    这些衣服男女都可以穿。

  17. variant 变体

    There are many colas on the market now, all variants on the original drink.

    现在市场上有多种可乐，都跟最初的差别不大。

  18. malaria/məˈler.i.ə/ 疟疾

    The doctor diagnosed his illness as malaria.

  19. criteria(kraɪˈtɪriə) 标准

    The Health Service should not be judged by financial criteria alone.

    公共医疗卫生服务不应仅仅以财务状况的好坏为标准来评判。

  20. game pad 游戏手柄

    speaker 音响
