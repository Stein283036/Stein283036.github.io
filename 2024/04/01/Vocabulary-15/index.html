Vocabulary 15

本文最后更新于 2024年4月1日 下午

  1. housing demolition/ˌdem.əˈlɪʃ.ən/ and relocation 拆迁

    Housing demolition and relocation is a way to get rich quickly.

  2. slang/slæŋ/

    俚语

    “Chicken” is slang for someone who isn’t very brave.

    chicken 是用来称呼胆小者的俚语。

  3. billionaire business magnate/ˈmaɡneɪt/ 亿万富翁商业巨头

  4. acne pit 痘坑

  5. hyperpigmentation/ˌhaɪ.pɚ.pɪɡ.mənˈteɪ.ʃən/ 色素沉着

    Hyperpigmentation may be caused by certain drugs.

    某些药物可能会引起色素沉着过度。

    Freshly waxed skin is prone to hyperpigmentation if it’s not protected with sunscreen.

    刚打过蜡的皮肤如果没有防晒霜的保护，很容易出现色素沉着过度。

  6. acne marks 痘印

  7. pimple 痘痘

  8. expedition/ˌek.spəˈdɪʃ.ən/

    远征；探险，考察

    Scott died while he was on an expedition to the Antarctic in 1912.

    斯科特 1912 年死于南极探险途中。

    The British expedition to Mount Everest is leaving next month.

    英国珠穆朗玛峰探险队将于下月启程。

  9. locker room 更衣室

  10. magician 魔术师

  11. semifinal

    半决赛

    She’s the youngest player ever to get through to/advance to a semifinal.

    她是闯入半决赛的最年轻的选手。

    The semifinal will be played on Saturday.

  12. orgasm /ˈɔːr.ɡæz.əm/

    to have an orgasm

    出现性高潮

    to achieve/reach orgasm

    达到性高潮

  13. prerequisite /ˌpriːˈrek.wə.zɪt/

    Passing a written test is a prerequisite for taking the advanced course.

    通过笔试是学习高级课程的前提。

    Public support is a prerequisite for/to the success of this project.

    公众支持是这一计划取得成功的必备条件。

    They had to agree to certain conditions as a prerequisite of being lent the money.

    他们不得不同意一些条件才能借到钱。

  14. compelling 引人注目的；令人信服的，有说服力的
    compelling evidence
    I found the whole film very compelling.
    a compelling story 扣人心弦的故事

  15. dump
    The police finally found the stolen car, which had been **dumped** near the beach.
    警方最终找到了被盗的汽车，该汽车被丢弃在海滩附近。

    That guy is really a jerk. I’m glad Lucy **dumped** him.
    那家伙实在是个混蛋。我很高兴露西甩了他。

    Mom asked me to take the garbage bag to the **dump**.

  16. total

    A total of seven players left the team.

    共有七名球员离开球队。

    Seven players left the team in total.

    The total number was over two thousand.

    The old man was a total stranger to me.

    The company’s losses totaled $105 million last year.

    I totaled up the scores with a calculator.

  17. evidence

    证据

    We have found a lot of evidence against John.

    我们找到了很多对约翰不利的证据。

    The king loves the queen very much, as evidenced by a large palace he built for her.

    国王非常爱王后，他为她建造了一座巨大的宫殿就证明了这一点。

  18. coffeemaker 咖啡机

  19. counter-intuitive 反直觉的

    Steering a sailboat is counter-intuitive - you push the tiller the opposite way to the way you want to go.

    驾驶帆船是违反直觉的——你将_舵柄_推向与你想要行驶的方向相反的方向。

  20. radically /ˈræd.ɪ.kəl.i/ completely or extremely

    完全地;极端地

    The accident radically changed the course of his life.

    Our approach is radically different to that of our competitors.
